how does it work?

Steadlly stores the contract between companies and empolyees. A contract within Steadlly works like any other contract. Except for one addition. Steadlly notes your job title and includes the skills neccesery for that job. Whenever your contract ends, your skills will be confurmed or denied by your employer.

This data is stored to repricent your personal CV, the only data we ask from you is a mobile number(for your convinience). so you are basicly anominous, within our system. Making it truelly about your skills as a person.
